🍪 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, font size, and other display preferences) over a period of time, so you don’t have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another. At Quiet Leaf Corner, we believe in a transparent and unhurried digital experience — like a peaceful walk through the garden.

📜 How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: These are strictly necessary to provide you with services available through our website and to use some of its features, such as access to secure areas, form submissions (contact, workshop registration), and core navigation. Without these cookies, services you’ve asked for cannot be provided.
  • Preference Cookies: Also known as "functionality cookies," these allow the website to remember choices you make (like your preferred plant hardiness zone or favorite blog categories) to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: We gather anonymous data via tools like a privacy-friendly analytics tracker (similar to Plausible or self-hosted) to understand which articles resonate, workshop signup flow, and overall garden community engagement. All data is aggregated and never sold.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used only with your consent, these cookies help deliver relevant gardening content, local nursery offers, and social media integration. They may be set by our social features (Instagram feed, Pinterest pins) after you interact with them.

🌿 Third-Party Cookies & Embedded Content

Our site may include embedded content from trusted partners (e.g., gardening tutorial videos from YouTube, map directions, or nature soundscapes). These third-party services may set cookies on your device if you interact with the embedded content. We only include such features when they add value to your plant-loving journey. Our preference panel allows you to block non-essential third-party cookies.

⏳ Cookie Duration & Management

We use both session cookies (which expire once you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain until their expiration date or until you delete them). You can manage or delete cookies at any time via your browser settings. However, please note that disabling essential cookies may impact site functionality — like being unable to send a contact form or book a workshop spot. For a harmonious browsing experience, we encourage you to adjust your preferences via the panel above or your browser’s privacy controls.

📅 Updates to This Cookies Policy

The "last updated" date will always be visible at the bottom of this page. Significant changes will be notified via a subtle banner or leaflet icon on our homepage. We encourage you to review this policy occasionally — just as you’d check the watering needs of your favorite fern.
