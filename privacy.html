🍃 Privacy Policy GDPR & CCPA ready

Effective date: March 30, 2026 | Last reviewed: March 30, 2026

At Quiet Leaf Corner (“we,” “us,” “our”), we believe that privacy is like rich soil — essential for trust to grow. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website quietleafcorner.com (the “Site”), interact with our gardening content, subscribe to newsletters, or engage with our community. We are committed to transparency and handling your personal data with the same gentle care we bring to tending heirloom tomatoes.

By using the Site, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using our services. Your privacy rights matter to us, and we comply with applicable laws including the GDPR (for European Economic Area residents) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to nurture our gardening community and improve your experience. The categories include:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, postal address (if you sign up for seed exchanges or donations), username, and any details you voluntarily share in comments or community forums.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs. This helps us understand which gardening guides are most beloved.
  • Communication Data: Any correspondence you send via contact forms, email inquiries, or newsletter interactions.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: We use cookies to remember preferences, analyze traffic, and personalize content — see our Cookies Policy for details.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data (like precise geolocation, health data, or financial information) unless you explicitly provide it for a specific purpose (e.g., donation processing through secure third parties).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data only for legitimate purposes aligned with our mission:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the website — making gardening inspiration bloom.
  • To send you newsletters, seasonal planting tips, or updates if you subscribed (you can opt out anytime).
  • To respond to inquiries, comments, and support requests with kindness and efficiency.
  • To moderate community forums and prevent spam or abusive behavior.
  • To analyze site trends via aggregated, anonymized data to understand what content helps gardeners most.
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce our Terms of Use.

We never sell your personal data to third parties. Period. No data harvesting for profit — only respectful digital stewardship.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR compliance)

If you reside in the European Economic Area, our processing of your personal data is based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you subscribe to our newsletter, accept cookies, or voluntarily provide information.
  • Contractual Necessity: To provide services you request (e.g., participation in a seed swap).
  • Legitimate Interests: Improving site security, analytics, and community management, always balancing your rights.
  • Legal Obligation: When required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Sharing & Disclosure of Information

We do not rent or sell your personal data. However, we may share limited information in the following contexts:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties that help operate our website, email platform (e.g., Mailchimp or similar), analytics (like Google Analytics anonymized), and secure donation processors. They are contractually obligated to protect your data and cannot use it for other purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect the safety, rights, or property of Quiet Leaf Corner, our community, or the public.
  • Community Interactions: When you voluntarily post comments or forum content, your username and message may be visible to other users. Please exercise discretion.
  • Business Transitions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred with notice posted on the site.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience — for instance, remembering your preferences or helping us understand which gardening articles resonate most. You can manage your cookie preferences via browser settings or our cookie consent tool. For detailed information, please see our separate Cookies Policy.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include SSL encryption, regular security reviews, and restricted access to personal information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure; we encourage you to use strong, unique credentials for any account you create.

7. Your Privacy Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”): Request deletion of your data, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If processing relies on consent, you may withdraw it anytime.

To exercise any rights, please contact us at privacy@quietleafcorner.com or use our mailing address below. We will respond within 30 days. California residents: Under CCPA, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of any “sale” of data — we do not sell personal data, so no opt-out needed. For more details, email us.

8. Children’s Privacy

Quiet Leaf Corner is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us, and we will promptly delete such information. We encourage families to explore gardening together with supervision.

9. International Data Transfers

Our website is hosted in the United States. If you access the Site from outside the US, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US, where data protection laws may differ. By using the Site, you acknowledge this transfer. For European users, we ensure appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) when transferring data outside the EEA.

10. Third-Party Links

Our content may include links to external seed suppliers, gardening blogs, or educational resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before interacting.

11. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. For example, newsletter subscriber data is kept until you unsubscribe. Comment data may be retained to maintain community integrity. Anonymized analytics data may be retained longer for trend analysis.

12. Email Marketing & Newsletters

If you subscribe to our newsletter (e.g., “The Gentle Gardener’s Dispatch”), we use your email address to send seasonal tips, new article highlights, and community updates. You can unsubscribe at any time via the “unsubscribe” link in each email. We use reputable email platforms that comply with data protection laws.

13. Donations & Financial Information

If you choose to support Quiet Leaf Corner with a donation, transactions are processed via trusted third-party payment processors (like Stripe or PayPal). We do not store full payment card details on our servers. Any financial information is collected solely for the purpose of processing your donation and is subject to the processor’s privacy policy.

14. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers offer a “Do Not Track” (DNT) feature. Our website currently does not respond to DNT signals because no uniform standard has been adopted. However, we honor your privacy choices through cookie consent tools and data access rights.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in practices, technology, or legal requirements. The revised version will be indicated by an updated “Effective Date” and posted on this page. We encourage you to review it periodically. If material changes are made, we will provide notice via website banner or email (for subscribers). Your continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance.

🌱 Your trust grows with us
Gardening is an act of patience, and so is data protection. If you have any questions, requests, or simply want to say hello, please contact our Data Steward at privacy@quietleafcorner.com or write to the address in the footer. We’re committed to cultivating a safe, respectful digital garden.
